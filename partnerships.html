Investment & Collaboration

Investment and Partnership Platform

The Foundation provides a structured platform for strategic collaboration, co-investment, and accountable programme implementation within the Zambezi Region.

Why Partner With the Foundation?

  • Direct, measurable community impact in the Zambezi Region
  • Alignment with Namibia’s national and regional development priorities
  • Transparent governance and accountable programme delivery
  • Opportunities for blended social and economic returns

Priority Areas for Collaboration

  • Agriculture and agri-processing development
  • Youth entrepreneurship funds and incubation hubs
  • Community infrastructure and social services
  • Sports and cultural development initiatives
  • Women-led enterprise development programmes

Partnership Modalities

Strategic investment partnerships
Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) collaboration
Development agency cooperation
Philanthropic and grant funding
