About

A Foundation built on unity, dignity, and opportunity.

The Dorothy Kabula Welfare Foundation is a non-profit organisation committed to uplifting communities across the Zambezi Region.

Legal Status

Non-Profit Making Association Incorporated under Section 21 and registered in accordance with the laws and governance framework of Namibia.

Background

Mobilising communities and partners toward inclusive regional growth, social impact, and sustainable livelihoods.

VisionA united, empowered, and economically thriving Zambezi Region.
MissionMobilise partnerships and resources to advance welfare, entrepreneurship, agriculture, and cultural heritage.
ValuesUnity • Integrity • Sustainability • Collaboration • Respect

Dorothy Kabula Welfare Foundation

A registered Namibian non-profit organisation committed to inclusive social and economic development across the Zambezi Region through community empowerment, entrepreneurship, agriculture, and strategic partnerships.
