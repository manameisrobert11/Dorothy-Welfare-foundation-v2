About

A Foundation built on unity, dignity, and opportunity.

The Dorothy Kabula Welfare Foundation is a non-profit organisation committed to uplifting communities across the Zambezi Region.

Partner with us Donate

Leadership

Hon. Dorothy Mareka Kabula

Founder and Chairperson

Founder and Chairperson of the Foundation and the first female Governor of the Zambezi Region. Her leadership reflects a lifelong commitment to community upliftment, gender equality, youth empowerment, and inclusive economic participation.

Legal Status

Non-Profit Making Association Incorporated under Section 21 and registered in accordance with the laws and governance framework of Namibia.

Background

Advancing unity, dignity, and sustainable socio-economic development.

The Foundation was established to promote unity, dignity, and sustainable socio-economic development within the Zambezi Region. It serves as a collaborative platform that mobilises communities, government institutions, the private sector, investors, and development partners toward inclusive regional growth and long-term social impact.

Vision A united, empowered, and economically thriving Zambezi Region.
Mission Mobilise partnerships and resources to advance welfare, entrepreneurship, agriculture, and cultural heritage.
Values Unity • Integrity • Sustainability • Collaboration • Respect
